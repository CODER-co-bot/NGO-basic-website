About Us

InAmigos Foundation was founded on September 23, 2020, by Mr. Govind Shukla (Founder & CEO). It is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization, licensed by the Central Government. It has its base at Chhattisgarh. It holds 80G & 12A certifications, ensuring transparency, accountability and tax-exempt benefits for donors. Our foundation is also CSR-1 registered, allowing us to collaborate with corporate partners for impactful Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) initiatives.

An NGO (non-governmental organization) is an independent, non-governmental entity dedicated to serving the public good. While typically nonprofit, NGOs can operate at local, regional, national, or international levels to tackle issues such as education, healthcare, human rights, environmental protection, and economic development. Although independent of government control, they may receive public funding and often overlap with the broader category of civil society organizations; their work ranges from grassroots community groups to large advocacy bodies and professional associations.

We are a registered Section 8 nonprofit, certified under 80G and 12A, listed on NGO Darpan (NITI Aayog), holder of CSR-1 registration and ISO 9001:2015 certification. Committed to fostering community development and social empowerment, we focus on bridging gaps in education, healthcare, and livelihood opportunities for underprivileged populations. Our programs combine immediate support with sustainable strategies designed to create measurable, long-term impact across the communities we serve.

Guided by inclusivity, empathy, and collaboration, our holistic initiatives include Bachpanshala (educational programs), Udaan (women’s empowerment), Prakriti (environmental sustainability), Jeev (animal care), and Seva (community support services). Each program is tailored to meet local needs, build capacity, and promote resilience—empowering individuals and families to achieve lasting social and economic progress.
